Learning Japanese

Welcome to my site! Here, I will try to teach you the basics the of Japanese alphabet! The Japanese language can be divided into 3 different writing methods.

The most basic of them is Hiragana, the first group of the Japanese alphabet. Most words in Japanese can be expressed in Hiragana, and will be the cornerstone in learning the language.

The second group of Japanese writing is Katakana, which deals mainly, but not limited to, foreign words that were not originally in the Japanese language.

Lastly, there is Kanji, which are Chinese characters adopted into the Japanese language from long ago. In general, Kanji characters keep the saming meaning in the Chinese language, but most are pronounced and expressed completely different in Japanese.

However, this site will only focus on Hiragana and Katakana
